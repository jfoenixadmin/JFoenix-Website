CCT International

 CCT is a leading multinational solutions provider specialized in construction and engineering information management & 3D project control. CCT has partnered with many leading research institutes around the world including Stanford and the American University of Beirut, as well as active membership in industry consortiums such as FIATECH, AIIM and various BIM initiatives around the world. CCT specializes in providing: Construction Project Control Systems & Consulting, Construction BIM & QA/QC 3D/5D, Isometric Detailing solutions, Document Management Systems, Construction Simulation Support, Handheld Mobile Computing Technologies, ERP Consulting & Implementation, Laser Scanning services and Mobile Fuel Consumption Systems.
